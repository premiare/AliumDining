about

Alium Dining is a creative and collaborative venue that combines the goodness of food with the love of people and community.
Our desire is to welcome, nurture and connect people through food, generosity, honesty and hospitality.
Alium pushes boundaries and inspires new thinking, creating a hub of learning, sharing and delicious new ideas.
